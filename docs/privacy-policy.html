Privacy Policy

CountMeIn Pro — Last updated: February 23, 2026

We believe in transparency. CountMeIn Pro collects only the minimum data needed to help you organize events and manage signups. We never sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our services:

Account Information (Organizers): When you create an organizer account, we collect your email address and password. Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text.

Event Information: When you create an event, we store the event name, description, date(s), location, capacity, and signup slot details you provide.

Participant Information: When participants sign up for an event, we collect their name, email address, and optionally their phone number. Participants do not need to create an account.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience, such as which features are most used. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Create and manage your organizer account
  • Display event details and signup information to organizers and participants
  • Send email reminders about upcoming events (only when enabled by the organizer)
  • Enable features like CSV export, calendar syncing, and slot swapping
  • Improve the app experience through anonymous usage analytics

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Event Organizers: Participant names, emails, and phone numbers are visible to the event organizer for the purpose of managing their event.
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for database hosting and authentication, and Resend for sending email reminders. These providers process data on our behalf under strict privacy agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or government request.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides enterprise-grade encryption at rest and in transit. We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication, and database-level access controls.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Event data and participant signups are retained until the organizer deletes the event. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Export: Export your event and signup data using the CSV export feature
  • Opt-out: Disable email reminders at any time

7. Children's Privacy

CountMeIn Pro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party services such as Google Calendar, Outlook, and Apple Calendar for the calendar syncing feature. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date above.
